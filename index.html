This is how it works

Order groceries

Farm is a circular service that ensures your kitchen is always stocked with the essentials. You browse our seasonal selection, choose your items, and place your order online.
See our stock here

Stock up

Once your order arrives, unpack the produce and store it where it suits you best. Keep your old containers—they’re yours to keep until your next order.

Scan & reorder

Ready for a refill? Grab the containers from your last order and scan the QR code with our app or your phone’s camera. This instantly adds the items back to your cart, making reordering a breeze.

Swap & reuse

When we deliver your next order, simply leave any empty containers outside. We’ll pick them up, clean them thoroughly, and reuse them for future deliveries.
